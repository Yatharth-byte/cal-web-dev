How to convert kWh to Joules

1 Joules is equal to 3600000 kWh (Kilowatt-hour) :

1 J = 2.7778 x 10-7 kWh = 1/3600000 kWh

Divide the X in Joules with 3600000 to get Y kWh

Y = X / 3600000
