How to convert kWh to Joules

1 kWh (Kilowatt-hour) is equal to 3600000 Joules:

1 kWh = 3600000 J = 3.6 x 106 = 3.6 MJ

Multiply the X in kWh with 3600000 to get Y Joules

Y = X * 3600000
