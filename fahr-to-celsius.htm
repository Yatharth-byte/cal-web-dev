How to convert fahrenheit to celsius

0 °Fahrenheit is equal to -17.778 °Celsius:

0 °F = -17.778 °C

Subtract 32 from X in fahrenheit and multiply by 1.8 to get Y celsius

Y = (X - 32) * 1.8  °F
